About Wheels n' Deals

Welcome to Wheels n' Deals, we serve our local community of skateboarders of all skill levels and experience.

Founded in 2021, Wheels n' Deals has been a staple for the local skaters to build their own boards, make repairs, as well as purchase pre-built skateboards of varying types.

We hope you enjoy our skateboards as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Wheels n' Deals Team
